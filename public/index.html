FAQ

Answers before you order

Can I upload photos during checkout?

Yes. You can upload one preview image while placing the order. You can also send more photos later on WhatsApp.

Will I get a preview before printing?

Yes. A preview can be confirmed before the final print for custom products.

How does payment work?

You can choose Pay on Delivery, UPI after Preview, or Pay at Store during checkout. The owner confirms payment status from the dashboard.

How long does delivery take?

Shipping usually takes 3 to 7 working days depending on your location.

Do you support bulk orders?

Yes. Bulk orders for events, offices, schools, and branding can be discussed through WhatsApp.
